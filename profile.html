Profile

Established in 1942

The Chief Education Office (CEO) Doda, initially the District Education Office since 1942, has transformed into a pivotal institution in Doda district's education system. Renamed in 1993, it oversees 1246 government and 271 private schools, playing a crucial role in policy implementation, resource management, and professional development for educators. Serving as a liaison between government and schools, the CEO Doda addresses challenges and fosters positive change, ensuring equitable access to quality education for all students in the region.
