Profile

Established in 1942

Established in 1942 as the District Education Office and renamed the Chief Education Office (CEO) Doda in 1993, this institution is central to Jammu and Kashmir's Doda district's education. Overseeing 1246 government and 271 private schools, it coordinates initiatives and implements policies from state and central governments. The CEO Doda ensures policy effectiveness through guideline dissemination, support provision, and progress monitoring. It manages resource allocation, including funds and infrastructure projects, to enhance education quality and promote inclusivity. Additionally, it facilitates teacher and staff professional development through training sessions, workshops, and seminars, aiming to improve teaching standards and enrich learning experiences. Furthermore, it serves as a platform for addressing educational challenges through dialogue and problem-solving, fostering positive change. In conclusion, the Chief Education Office Doda remains pivotal in the district's educational framework. Through its roles in administration, resource management, professional development, and advocacy, it ensures access to quality education for every child, shaping future generations.
