Director of School Education Jammu Conducts Comprehensive Review Meeting in Doda

Doda, May 28, 2024 â€“ To review the educational framework in the district, the Chief Education Office (CEO) Doda hosted a meeting chaired by the Director of School Education Jammu, Shri Ashok Kumar Sharma (JKAS). The meeting saw the presence of prominent education officials, including the Joint Director School Education Doda-Kishtwar-Ramban, Principal DIET, District Education Planning Officer (DEPO), Principals of Higher Secondary Schools, and Zonal Education Officers (ZEOs).

Emphasis on Enrolment and Gender Parity

The Director's review covered various facets of the educational progress in the district, with a particular emphasis on increasing student enrolment. A key focus area highlighted by Shri Ashok Kumar Sharma was the imperative need to boost the enrolment of girls in secondary and higher secondary schools. He urged the educational leaders to implement strategic initiatives and community outreach programs aimed at encouraging more girls to pursue higher education, thereby contributing to gender parity and empowerment through education.v

JK Smart Attendance App: Ensuring Accountability

An important agenda of the meeting was the effective implementation of the JK Smart Attendance App. Shri Ashok Kumar Sharma stressed the necessity of daily attendance marking via the app, underlining its role in fostering accountability and ensuring real-time monitoring of staff attendance. He directed the CEO Doda to enforce strict measures against employees failing to comply with this mandate, thus reinforcing the commitment to transparency and efficiency within the educational system.

Rationalization Process and Staff Allocation

The Director instructed the CEO to complete the rationalization process immediately after the model code of conduct ends. He stressed the importance of ensuring adequate staff in schools, particularly those in remote areas and those currently facing a shortage of teaching personnel. This directive aims to guarantee that all schools, regardless of location, have the necessary resources and staff to provide quality education.

Launch of CEO Doda Website

In a stride towards digital advancement, the Director officially launched the new website for the CEO Doda. Developed by the in-house IT team, this digital platform is poised to streamline administrative processes and enhance communication between the education office and the stakeholders. Shri Ashok Kumar Sharma lauded the technical team for their commendable work in designing the user-friendly and informative website, marking a significant milestone in the district's journey towards digital integration in education administration.

Addressing Ground-Level Issues

During the meeting, the Director also took the time to listen to the challenges faced by Principals and ZEOs at the ground level. He assured them that their concerns would be addressed with impactful solutions, demonstrating a commitment to resolving issues that hinder the effective functioning of schools and educational offices.

Looking Ahead

The review meeting underscored the Director's vision of an inclusive and accountable education system, setting a clear agenda for the educational authorities in Doda. With a focus on increasing enrolment, particularly of girls, ensuring strict adherence to attendance protocols, and leveraging digital tools for administrative efficiency, the district is set to witness substantial improvements in its educational landscape. This meeting not only highlighted the progress made so far but also set the stage for future initiatives aimed at elevating the standard of education in Doda. The collaborative efforts of all education officials, guided by the insightful directives of Shri Ashok Kumar Sharma, promise to pave the way for a brighter and more educated future for the district.
